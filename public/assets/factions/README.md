# Faction Art

Faction artwork is packaged as one replaceable image per faction.

```text
uk.png         United Kingdom faction preview art
germany.png    Germany faction preview art
france.png     France faction preview art
italy.png      Italy faction preview art
russia.png     Russia faction preview art
japan.png      Japan faction preview art
usa.png        United States faction preview art
australia.png  Australia faction preview art
```

Add future faction images using `<faction-id>.png`, then set the faction's `art` field in `src/game/cards.js`.
